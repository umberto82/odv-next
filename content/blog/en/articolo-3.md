Veneto is one of the most versatile regions in Italy: it offers world-class art cities, hills covered with vineyards, natural thermal springs, mountains, and sea. If you are planning a holiday in Veneto, the best choice is a villa with private pool in the Euganean Hills, right in the heart of the region.

## Why Choose the Euganean Hills for Your Veneto Holiday

The Euganean Hills are a green island in the heart of Veneto, just a few kilometers from Padua. Here time slows down among vineyards, olive groves, and ancient villages. It is the perfect destination for those seeking a relaxing vacation without giving up cultural excursions and outdoor activities.

Unlike other crowded tourist destinations, the Euganean Hills offer an authentic and genuine experience, away from mass tourism.

## What to See During Your Holiday in Veneto

From the villa you can easily reach:

- **Padua** (25 min) — Giotto's Scrovegni Chapel, Prato della Valle, Basilica of St. Anthony
- **Venice** (35 min) — St. Mark's Square, Grand Canal, Rialto Bridge
- **Verona** (35 min) — Roman Arena, Juliet's House, Castelvecchio
- **Vicenza** (25 min) — Palladian Basilica, Olympic Theatre, Villa Rotonda
- **Abano Terme and Montegrotto** (15 min) — natural thermal baths and wellness centers
- **Dolomites** (2 hours) — hiking, trekking, mountain air

## Holidays in Veneto for Families

If you are traveling with children, the Euganean Hills are the ideal base. Adventure parks, wildlife parks, thermal baths with children's pools, and art cities offer fun for all ages. After a day of excursions, the villa's private pool is the perfect reward for both adults and children.

Veneto is also the land of Prosecco, Amarone, and the wines of the Euganean Hills. During your stay, you can visit wineries, participate in tastings, and discover the authentic flavors of Venetian cuisine in the area's restaurants and trattorias.
