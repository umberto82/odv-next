Veneto is one of the most versatile regions in Italy: it offers sea, mountains, art cities, and hilly landscapes immersed in greenery. If you are planning your holidays in Veneto, here is a complete guide on where to go, what to see, and how to live an unforgettable experience, even with children.

## Mountains in Veneto: Nature and Relaxation between Dolomites and Plateau

If you dream of a mountain holiday in Veneto, the Belluno Dolomites are a perfect choice for hikes, fresh air, and breathtaking views.

Among the most popular locations:
- Cortina d'Ampezzo
- Auronzo di Cadore
- Asiago Plateau (ideal with children)

These areas offer refuges, walks suitable for all ages, and plenty of greenery.

---

## Sea in Veneto: Sun, Beaches, and Fun

Veneto is bathed by the Adriatic Sea and offers many seaside destinations ideal for families and young people.

- Bibione and Jesolo, with equipped beach establishments and entertainment
- Sottomarina, quieter and suitable for relaxation
- Chioggia, the "little Venice"

---

## Holidays in Veneto with Children

The perfect mix is nature + activities:

- Colli Euganei Wildlife Park
- Adventure Park Le Fiorine (Teolo)
- Museum of Nature and Mankind in Padua
- Abano and Montegrotto Thermal Baths

A holiday home immersed in the Euganean Hills is ideal for relaxing after days of exploration.

---

## Holidays in Veneto 2025: What to Expect

2025 will bring cultural events, wine festivals, and gastronomic fairs.

You can choose between:
- nature and relaxation in the Euganean Hills
- mountain excursions
- sea and beaches
- art cities
- family experiences
