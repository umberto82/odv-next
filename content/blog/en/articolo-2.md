The Euganean Hills are one of the most fascinating — and still little-known — areas of Northern Italy. A cluster of gentle volcanic hills rising suddenly from the Veneto plain, just a few kilometers from Padua, offering surprising landscapes and an atmosphere of authentic tranquility.

Perfect for those seeking a nature-filled vacation with slow rhythms, genuine experiences, and family hospitality, the Euganean Hills are an ideal destination in every season.

## Nature and Silence to Recharge

If your goal is to escape the routine, the Euganean Hills are an oasis of quiet. Shaded trails wind through woods, vineyards, and olive groves, offering relaxing views and breathtaking sunsets. Every hike, on foot or by bike, is an opportunity to slow down and reconnect with nature.

## Thermal Baths and Wellness

The area is renowned for its natural hot springs, particularly those of Abano and Montegrotto, among the oldest in Europe. Here you can dedicate yourself to absolute relaxation with thermal treatments, warm pools, and wellness centers immersed in greenery.
